**Read this before using niw-guru.**

## Not legal advice

niw-guru is software that generates **draft work product and research aids**. It is **not** a law
firm, is **not** a lawyer, and does **not** provide legal advice, legal opinions, or legal
representation. Using niw-guru does **not** create an attorney–client relationship with anyone.

Immigration law — including the EB-2 National Interest Waiver standard — is complex,
fact-specific, and changes over time. The outputs niw-guru produces are starting points for your
own work, not a filed petition and not a substitute for professional judgment.

## Have an attorney review your petition

**Before you file anything with USCIS, have a licensed U.S. immigration attorney (or an
accredited representative) review your petition and supporting evidence.** Only a qualified
professional can assess your specific case.

## Verify everything

Although niw-guru is designed around evidence traceability and a "zero unverified claims" rule,
**AI systems can still make mistakes.** Outputs may contain errors, misquotes, wrong page
references, outdated policy, or misread sources. You are responsible for independently verifying:

- every quote against the downloaded source document,
- every page number and citation,
- every factual claim about your background, and
- the current state of USCIS policy (re-check anything dated in `knowledge/`).

Items niw-guru could not verify are marked `[VERIFY]`; check them all.
